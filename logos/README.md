# Archolith Top Five Logo Candidates

These are the five logo directions I would carry forward from the concept work.

## Ranking

1. `01-k8-dropping-keystone.svg`
   Narrative strongest. Feels specific to Archolith because the keystone is being seated into the structure.
2. `02-k2-minimal-keystone.svg`
   Best for favicon-scale use. Simplest and most durable small-size mark.
3. `03-k1-classic-keystone.svg`
   Safest serious primary mark. Timeless and structurally legible.
4. `04-doorway.svg`
   Best non-keystone branch. Strong negative-space presence and more mystery.
5. `05-strata-column.svg`
   Best supporting-system mark. Fits the layered-context story, though weaker as a primary logo.

## Notes

- The keystone family is still the strongest overall direction.
- The split/fracture experiments were useful, but they did not beat the arch/keystone marks.
- If a final refinement round happens, I would combine:
  - the structural clarity of `K1` / `K2` / `K8`
  - the irregularity instinct from the later wedge explorations
  - without losing the load-bearing arch read.
